Standard SendKeys
1. a and b are pressed, send the string argument	(ab)
2. To Send same letter multiple times				{a 10}
3. To Send ALT Tab									%{TAB}
4. To Send ALT and space							%{ }
5. TO Send char										(a)
6. Right Click of Mouse								+{F10}
